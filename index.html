The Function Stack Test

Carl Jung proposed that the mind runs on four functions — two ways of deciding and two ways of perceiving. We all use all four, but in a personal order, or stack. This test finds yours.

Thinking — logic & analysis Feeling — values & people Sensing — facts & the present Intuition — patterns & possibility

Thinking opposes Feeling, and Sensing opposes Intuition — so your strongest function and your weakest are always a pair. The test ranks all four into your stack, adds whether you lean introverted or extraverted, and names which of Jung's 8 classic types you lead with — with guidance.

⏱️ ~5 minutes 📋 28 questions 🔒 100% private — nothing leaves your browser

Just pick the answer that feels most like you — not the one that sounds best. Option order is shuffled so you can't game it. This is a reflective tool grounded in Jungian typology, not a clinical instrument.
