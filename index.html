The Function Stack Test

A forced-choice test that measures how four Jungian cognitive functions are stacked in your mind — and what that ordering says about how you think, decide, and grow.

Ni · Introverted Intuition Fe · Extraverted Feeling Se · Extraverted Sensing Ti · Introverted Thinking

Each question shows four ways of responding to the same situation. Just pick the one that feels most like you — not the one that sounds best. There are no right answers, and the order of options is shuffled so you can't game it.

⏱️ ~5 minutes 📋 28 questions 🔒 100% private — nothing leaves your browser

These four functions, depending on their order, describe four type families: INFJ, ENFJ, ESTP and ISTP. This is a reflective tool grounded in Jungian / MBTI® function theory, not a clinical instrument.
